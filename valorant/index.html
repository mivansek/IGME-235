Valorant


Publishing Info
Published by: Riot Games, Inc.
Developed by: Riot Games Hong Kong SAR, Riot Games, Inc.
Released: June 2, 2020

Description

Valorant is an objective based online multiplayer game where two teams of five players face each other in a series of rounds, switching roles as defending and attacking teams around planting and defusing a bomb called the "spike", similar to the gameplay of Counter-Strike. The first team to win thirteen rounds, wins the match. The game rewards precise accuracy and fast reflexes, team work and a tactical approach. The defending team also wins the round if 100 seconds pass without a successful plant by the attacking team.

Game Categories

Genre: Action
Perspective: 1st person
Gameplay: Shooter
